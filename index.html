Above are examples of magazine covers that Cipe Pineles designed at each magazine company she worked at. Pineles use of color and accent color always caught my eye and is just so visually pleasing.

Cipe Pineles is known as one of the most influential art designers of the twentieth century. Not only was she the first female art director of many major magazines, but she is also credited as the first person to infuse fine art into mainstream media. One of the things that I always admire about Pineles beside all of her success is how she thought of design, making sure to not get lost in a fantasy. Making reality and current events appealing or at least visually intriguing to spread information and art to readers is an admirable skill.
